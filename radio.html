hack club radio!!!


what is this?

a small idea i had where members of hack club could suggest songs to be played in one radio station

how do i get one of my favorite songs on here?

dm ionixv on slack with the name and link to your song

how does it work?

i have a homelab running azuracast, a open source radio station manager

i take your request and put it in the main playlist, very simple indeed. the playlist loops until something stops it

the radio station will run as long as the homelab is, which is most of the time unless the cats unplug it

if the radio station is down, dm me!!!!
